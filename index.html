ELISA · Curve Analyst

A professional-grade standard curve fitting and back-calculation platform for ELISA assays. Import your reference standards, choose a regression model, and instantly quantify unknown samples — all in the browser with no data leaving your machine.

01
Reference StandardsPaste concentration & OD values, select a curve model, then click "Fit Reference Curve".
02
Choose a ModelLinear, 4PL/5PL sigmoidal, polynomial, spline & more. R² and RMSE guide your choice.
03
Sample QuantificationPaste unknown sample absorbances — concentrations are back-calculated automatically.
04
Export ResultsDownload as Excel, CSV, TSV or JSON, or copy the table directly into your spreadsheet.
⚡ All processing runs locally — no data is transmitted
